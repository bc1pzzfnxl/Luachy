# Future Integrations & Features Roadmap

This list categorizes potential enhancements for **Luachy**, inspired by modern PKM (Personal Knowledge Management) trends (2024-2026) like Obsidian, Tana, and Notion.

### 1. Capture & Input
- **Voice-to-Memo (AI):** Instant transcription of spoken thoughts into Markdown.
- **Web Clipper:** Browser extension to send URLs or text snippets directly to the memo stream.
- **Local OCR:** Drag an image into a memo to automatically extract text via local processing.

### 2. Networking & Structure
- **Bi-directional Linking:** Use `[[Card Name]]` syntax to link memos and Kanban cards interactively.
- **Supertags (Metadata):** Add tags like `#project/active` to automatically trigger specific schemas (due dates, priority).
- **Block References:** Quote specific memos inside Kanban cards without duplicating data.

### 3. Visualization & Analysis
- **Graph View:** Interactive 3D/2D visualization of links between thoughts and tasks.
- **Activity Heatmap:** Year-at-a-glance view of capture and completion frequency.
- **Focus Mode:** A "Zen" writing interface with minimal UI and atmospheric soundscapes.

### 4. Workflow & Actionability
- **Spaced Repetition (SRL):** A review system that surfaces old, un-promoted memos to maintain knowledge retention.
- **Recurring Cards:** Automated creation of repeating tasks (e.g., "Weekly Review").
- **Checklist Progress:** Visual progress bars on Kanban cards derived from Markdown `- [ ]` lists.

### 5. Agentic AI (Local-First)
- **Auto-Promote Suggestion:** AI identifies task-like memos and suggests Kanban placement.
- **GraphRAG:** Ask questions to your local database (via Ollama) using your entire history as context.
- **Semantic Search:** Find notes based on meaning rather than just exact keywords.

### 6. Output & Export
- **Static Site Generator:** One-click publishing of selected memos to a personal blog.
- **Markdown Export:** Bulk export of the entire database into `.md` files for long-term portability.
